Things to do on christmas day

Visit the monumenst and memorials. Christmas is a great day to walk along the National Mall to view the monuments and memorials in Washington, D.C. You can still get up close and enjoy the beautiful views of the capital city. Stand on the steps of the Lincoln Memorial, the Jefferson Memorial, and more and take in the breathtaking views of the nation's capital.

Walk the Grounds of Arlington National Cemetery. The grounds to the national burial ground are open 365 days a year. Visitors may explore on foot. Visit the famous gravesites of notable Americans such as Presidents John F. Kennedy and William Howard Taft. See many memorials including the Coast Guard Memorial, the Space Shuttle Challenger Memorial, Spanish-American War Memorial, the USS Maine Memorial and many more.
